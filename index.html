Who We Are

A PLACE FOR EVERYONE

We’re a family. We know each of our students by name and make it a point to have personal contact with every parent. In our classes, your child is more than just another name on a roster.

SELF-ESTEEM BUILDERS

Everyone is special. We fight bullying and body image issues by showing each child their own worth. Your child will begin to feel confident when in our positive, nurturing classes.

FINDERS OF PASSION

Your child has the freedom to explore our many classes to find their passion. We want every student to be excited about what they’re learning and how they can express themselves.

PART OF THE COMMUNITY

We give back to our community with fundraisers and events for the needy. Your child can experience the joy in helping those in need, becoming more compassionate and caring.

Miss Gigi founded Step It Up Performing Arts in 1996 on the principles of fostering the gift of artistic performance in our community. Our focus is not on competition but on the total performance education and well-being of our students. While we don't focus on competition, we don't discourage it. Some of our students have auditioned and been accepted to Denver School of the Arts, The Russian Ballet,  and recently, an invitation to audition at Julliard. Step It Up Performing Arts has developed a unique and powerful dance and performance educational program for toddlers to adults. Our students not only learn great technique, they have a lot of fun as well.

ACTING

READ DESCRIPTION

Our acting classes are designed to help your child come out of their shell and gain confidence. By having to play a number of different roles, your child will also develop empathy and an understanding for others. Acting also helps public speaking skills as well!

BALLET

READ DESCRIPTION

We offer a wide range of ballet classes for children of all ages and skill levels. Our specialty is in pre-point, point conditioning, and pointe classes. Learning ballet is a fantastic way to stay fit and limber. Our ballet classes foster grace and coordination.

GYMNASTICS

READ DESCRIPTION

Our gymnastics courses will get your children stretching, leaping, tumbling, and flipping. Gymnastics is a great way to gain coordination and endurance. This can be learned on its own but is also a great class to take if you or your children are interested in moving into other dance options as well.

HIP-HOP

READ DESCRIPTION

We offer several hip-hop classes that will give your child an exciting outlet for creative personal expression. We offer several varieties including tricking and break dancing. This is a style of acrobatic dancing to fun upbeat music. It combines intricate footwork, pantomime, spinning head stands, and tumbling.

Our advanced class develops power, personal style, coordination, agility, confidence, focus, and memory all while having a blast.

COURSE SCHEDULE AND REGISTRATION

JAZZ

READ DESCRIPTION

Jazz dancing is a form of dance that showcases a dancer’s individual style and originality. Every jazz dancer interprets and executes moves and steps in their own way.

Our jazz classes offer an improvised and energetic style of exercise that allows students to physically express themselves through a range of routines. With flowing movements similar to ballet, jazz dancers combine sharp turns, large leaps and quick footwork in a show of flexibility and grace. Our jazz classes promote freedom of expression and individuality. Your children will also learn grace, poise, coordination, and balance through this course.

COURSE SCHEDULE AND REGISTRATION

PIANO ENSEMBLE

READ DESCRIPTION

Music lessons have been shown to improve a child’s performance in school. A study showed that after 8 months of piano lessons, preschoolers showed a 46% boost in their spatial IQ, which is crucial for higher brain functions such as complex mathematics. At Step It Up we not only teach the fundamentals of music but the joy of playing music as well.

TAP

READ DESCRIPTION

At Step It Up, our tap classes are designed to develop rhythm, style, and sound. Students will learn a variety of tap styles from Broadway to Rhythm Tap. Exercises focus on building flexibility of the knee and ankles, coordination, and speed of movement and having fun.
